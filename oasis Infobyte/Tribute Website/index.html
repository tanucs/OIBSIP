A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

APJ Abdul Kalam is one of the most celebrated men of India. He is beloved by everyone, as a president, as a scientist, as an inspiration, Abdul Kalam was a man of many talents. APJ Abdul Kalam’s contribution to Indian space research as an aerospace scientist is remarkable. He inspires lots of children of India to work hard and fulfill their dream. He became the 11th president of India in the year 2002 and served the country as the president for the next five years, that is to say, till 2007. We celebrate his birthday as "World Students’ Day." Ratna" in the year 1997.

  • Died: 27 July, 2015, Shillong, Meghalaya
